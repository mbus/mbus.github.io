MBus Legal Agreement

In Short:

  • If you use/implement MBus you must call it MBus
  • To market something as MBus compatible, you must adhere to the MBus spec
  • Commercial derivative works (e.g. MBus without the reserved arbitration edge) are prohibited
    • Non-commercial (e.g. academic) derivatives are fine (encouraged!)
    • If you have a substantive improvement / fix / etc, contact the MBus team and we will work it into the specification
